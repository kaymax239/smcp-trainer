# Deck Department Academy

Inspired by the structure of professional maritime deck training programs such as Warsash-style deck, STCW, ship handling, simulator, and bridge operations training.

This content is a planning structure only. It must be rewritten into original KAYMAX Maritime Academy lessons and missions.

## Level 1: Officer Cadet Foundation

Purpose: Introduce the cadet to life at sea, shipboard organization, watch routines, and professional conduct.

Lessons:
1. Life at Sea
2. Ship Organization
3. Ranks and Responsibilities
4. Maritime Culture and Discipline
5. Safety Culture on Board

Mission ideas:
- Mission 01: One Week Before Departure
- Mission 02: Meet the Bridge Team
- Mission 03: First Safety Briefing
- Mission 04: Emergency Muster Drill
- Mission 05: Shipboard Routine
