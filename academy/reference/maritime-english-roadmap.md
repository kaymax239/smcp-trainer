# Maritime English Roadmap

## Purpose

This document defines the academic structure of the SMCP Trainer Academy.

The roadmap is based on:

* STCW
* SMCP
* IMO Model Course 3.17 Maritime English
* OICNW Competencies

The objective is not language learning alone.

The objective is operational competence for maritime officers.

# Academy Design Rule

Every mission must support:

* STCW
* SMCP
* Maritime English
* Operational competence

The Academy must train maritime officers, not English students.
