# Mission 03: First Day on the Bridge

## Mission Brief

Vessel: MV Ocean Pioneer

Location: Bridge

Time: 0600

Role: Officer Cadet

It is your first morning assigned to the Bridge.

The Second Officer is conducting the morning watch.

Your task is to observe bridge operations and learn basic watchkeeping procedures.

Remember:

Observe first.

Speak professionally.

Stay alert.

# Watchkeeping Vocabulary

Lookout

A person assigned to observe surrounding traffic and hazards.

Watch

A period of duty on the bridge.

Course

The direction the vessel is steering.

Position

The vessel's location.

Heading

The direction the bow is pointing.

---

# Basic SMCP

Bridge Communication

* Contact on starboard side.
* Contact on port side.
* Vessel ahead.
* Vessel astern.
* Course steady.
* Alter course to port.
* Alter course to starboard.
* Maintain lookout.
