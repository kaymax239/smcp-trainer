# Mission Overview

The vessel is scheduled to depart in seven days.

Before departure, all departments must complete safety inspections, equipment checks, and operational readiness reviews.

The Officer Cadet will participate in the pre-departure briefing and assist the deck department.

# Scene 02

Character:
Captain David Jones

Briefing:

Good morning Officers and Cadets.

MV Ocean Pioneer is scheduled to depart in seven days.

Before departure, all departments must complete inspections, verify safety equipment, and review emergency procedures.

Safety remains our highest priority.

As Officer Cadets, you are expected to observe, learn, and report professionally.

Today you will assist the deck department during the initial inspection process.
