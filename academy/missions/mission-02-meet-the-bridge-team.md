# Mission 02: Meet the Bridge Team

## Mission Brief

Location: MV Ocean Pioneer

Time: First day onboard

Role: Officer Cadet

You arrived onboard yesterday.

Today, Captain David Jones has instructed the Chief Officer to introduce you to the bridge team.

Your objective is to meet the officers responsible for navigation and safety operations.

Listen carefully.

Observe everything.

Remember:

A good officer learns before giving orders.

---

# Scene 1

You arrive at the Bridge at 0800.

The bridge is quiet.

Navigation equipment is operating normally.

Several officers are already on duty.

A senior officer approaches you.

"Good morning, Cadet."

"My name is Michael Turner."

"I am the Chief Officer."

"Welcome onboard the MV Ocean Pioneer."

How do you respond?

A.
Good morning, Chief Officer.

B.
Hey.

C.
What's up?

Correct Answer:
A

---

# Chief Officer Introduction

The Chief Officer is responsible for:

* Cargo operations
* Deck crew management
* Safety equipment
* Emergency preparedness
* Training of cadets

The Chief Officer says:

"During your training you will work closely with me."

"You are expected to observe, learn, and ask professional questions."

---

# Scene 2

The Chief Officer points toward another officer.

"That is the Second Officer."

"He is responsible for navigation planning."

The officer approaches.

"Good morning, Cadet."

"My name is Sarah Mitchell."

"I am the Second Officer."

"I prepare voyage plans and navigation charts."

How do you respond?

A.
Good morning, Second Officer.

B.
Hello Captain.

C.
Good night.

Correct Answer:
A

---

# Second Officer Responsibilities

The Second Officer is responsible for:

* Passage planning
* Navigation charts
* ECDIS updates
* Publications
* Voyage preparation

Important Equipment:

* ECDIS
* Radar
* Charts
* GPS

"I am responsible for safety equipment."

# Third Officer Responsibilities

# Scene 4

The Chief Officer takes you outside onto the main deck.

A deck crew member is supervising maintenance work.

The Chief Officer says:

"This is Bosun Robert Hayes."

"He is the senior deck rating onboard."

The Bosun shakes your hand.

"Welcome onboard, Cadet."

How do you respond?

A.
Good morning, Bosun Hayes.

B.
Ignore him.

C.
Call him Captain.

Correct Answer:
A

---

# Bosun Responsibilities

The Bosun supervises:

* Deck maintenance
* Mooring operations
* Painting
* Chipping
* Deck crew activities

The Bosun has many years of practical experience.

Smart cadets learn from the Bosun.
