# Course 01: Introduction to Shipboard Life

School: Deck Department Academy

Level: Officer Cadet Foundation

Vessel: MV Ocean Pioneer

Captain: David Jones

Bosun: Robert Hayes

Student Role: Officer Cadet

---

## Course Purpose

Introduce the Officer Cadet to life at sea, shipboard organization, safety culture, discipline, and professional conduct.

The cadet learns through immersion in a working ship environment where English is the operational language.
