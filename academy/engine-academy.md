# Engine Department Academy

Inspired by the structure of professional maritime engineering training programs such as Great Lakes-style engine officer preparation, engine room familiarization, watchkeeping, machinery systems, and simulator-based emergency response.

This content is a planning structure only. It must be rewritten into original KAYMAX Maritime Academy lessons and missions.

## Level 1: Engine Cadet Foundation

### Course 01: Engine Room Familiarization

Purpose: Introduce the cadet to the engine department, machinery spaces, safety rules, and basic reporting.

Lessons:
1. Engine Room Layout
2. Engine Department Organization
3. Engine Room Safety
4. Personal Protective Equipment
5. Basic Engine Room Communication

Mission ideas:
- Report to Chief Engineer
- Identify engine room spaces
- Select correct PPE
- Complete engine room safety walkaround
